Privacy Policy

App: Gopnik from Chertanovo (com.chertanovo.gopnik) · Last updated: 11 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the mobile game Gopnik from Chertanovo (the "App") handles information. Please read it carefully.

1. Data we collect

We do not collect, transmit, store on any server, or share any personal data. The App has no user accounts, no login, and does not require an internet connection to play.

2. Data stored on your device

The App saves game progress locally on your device only, using Android's private app storage (SharedPreferences). This includes your high scores, in-game currency, purchased cosmetic items, and settings. This data never leaves your device and is deleted when you uninstall the App.

3. Permissions

The App requests no sensitive permissions. It does not access your contacts, location, camera, microphone, storage, phone state, or any other personal information.

4. Network, ads and analytics

The App contains no advertising, no third-party analytics, and no tracking SDKs. It makes no network requests. Because no data is collected, none is sold or shared with third parties.

5. Children

The App does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

6. Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this same URL with a new "Last updated" date.
