PRIVACY POLICY — GOPNIK FROM CHERTANOVO
========================================

App: Gopnik from Chertanovo (com.chertanovo.gopnik)
Last updated: 11 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the mobile game "Gopnik from Chertanovo"
(the "App") handles information. Please read it carefully.

1. DATA WE COLLECT
We do not collect, transmit, store on any server, or share any personal data.
The App has no user accounts, no login, and does not require an internet
connection to play.

2. DATA STORED ON YOUR DEVICE
The App saves game progress locally on your device only, using Android's private
app storage (SharedPreferences). This includes your high scores, in-game
currency, purchased cosmetic items, and settings. This data never leaves your
device and is deleted when you uninstall the App.

3. PERMISSIONS
The App requests no sensitive permissions. It does not access your contacts,
location, camera, microphone, storage, phone state, or any other personal
information.

4. NETWORK, ADS AND ANALYTICS
The App contains no advertising, no third-party analytics, and no tracking SDKs.
It makes no network requests. Because no data is collected, none is sold or
shared with third parties.

5. CHILDREN
The App does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including
children under the age of 13.

6. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY
If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted with a new
"Last updated" date.
